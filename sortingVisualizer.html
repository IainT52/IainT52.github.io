Quick tutorial
Speed:
Hover over the bars to control the speed of the algorithm and animation. Moving your mouse to the right will decrease the speed and moving to the left will increase it.
Suggestion: Move your mouse to the center (horizontally) of the bars. From there, move left or right to change the speed. Randomize:
Resets the size (width) of the bars and shuffles their heights.
Pause:
Press to play and press again to pause.
Note: You can pause and switch algorithms halfway through a sort.
