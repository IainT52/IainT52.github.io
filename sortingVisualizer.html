Quick tutorial
Speed:
Hover over the bars to conrtol the speed of the algorithm and animation. Moving your mouse to the right will decrease the speed while moving it to the left will increase the speed.
Randomize:
The randomize button resets the size (width) of the bars and shuffles their heights.
Pause:
The Pause button will pause the animation. You can press it again to resume the animation.
Note: You can pause and switch algorithms halfway through a sort.
