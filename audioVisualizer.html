Quick tutorial
Finding a song: Use the search bar below to 1. Enter a URL 2. Search for a song with a keyword search 3. Use the pre-selected URL and hit "Find Track".

Play: Press the "Find Track" button to search and play songs. You can pause and resume the music with the play/pause button.

Note: Any artists that require a SoundCloud Go+ subscription to listen to thier music do not allow their songs to be played through the API.
