Quick tutorial
You can immediately press the submit button to play the pre-selected Soundcloud track or you can click the Soundcloud button below to find your own track! The second method requires you to paste the url into the textbox below and hit submit.
Play:
Hit the submit button to find and play the selected song.
Note: Some artists on Soundcloud do not allow their songs to be played through the API. Specifically, an artist that requires a Soundcloud subscription (Go+) to listen to a song.
Format:
https://soundcloud.com/user/track
